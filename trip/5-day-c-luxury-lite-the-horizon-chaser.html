About This Safari

The Horizon Chaser is our most requested five-day luxury experience. Fly into the Mara and stay in an exclusive private conservancy camp for four nights with a certified senior guide. The centrepiece is a pre-dawn hot air balloon flight over the Mara plains — floating in silence above migrating herds and waking predators as the sun rises over East Africa. On the ground, private conservancy access means off-road game drives, night drives, and walking safaris unavailable in the main reserve.

Ideal for: Honeymoons, significant anniversaries, bucket-list travellers, premium experience seekers.

Day by Day

1

Fly into the Mara

Wilson Airport Nairobi to Mara airstrip — 45 minutes. Check in to your luxury conservancy camp. Afternoon game drive on private tracks. Sunset drinks on the plains.

2

Full Private Day

Dawn game drive — private conservancy access means you can follow a kill or stay at a sighting as long as needed. Return for full breakfast. Afternoon drive into golden hour. Night drive to experience the Mara after dark.

3

Hot Air Balloon Morning

Pre-dawn wake-up (5:00am). Transfer to balloon launch point. One hour flight over the Mara at sunrise — wildebeest herds, lion prides, the river and plains from 1,000 feet. Traditional champagne breakfast in the bush after landing. Afternoon game drive.

4

Full Day in the Bush

Full day game drives. Optional guided walking safari with armed escort — experience the Mara on foot, track wildlife, learn the ecosystem from ground level. Evening private dinner in the bush.

5

Final Drive & Fly Out

Last morning drive before the Nairobi flight. Champagne breakfast at camp. Airstrip transfer and fly back to Wilson Airport.
