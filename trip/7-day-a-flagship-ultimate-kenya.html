About This Safari

The Ultimate Kenya Circuit is the definitive Kenya safari. Three ecosystems across seven days: Amboseli for Kilimanjaro and elephants, Samburu for the rare northern species, and three nights in the Masai Mara on private conservancy tracks. Every transfer is by light aircraft — no long road drives, no wasted hours. This is Kenya done completely, done properly, done once and remembered forever.

Ideal for: Anyone wanting to see the full breadth of Kenya's wildlife in one trip. First-timers and experienced safari travellers equally.

Day by Day

1

Arrive Nairobi

Arrival day. Airport transfer to your Nairobi hotel. Briefing with your guide. Early rest before a dawn departure.

2

Nairobi to Amboseli

Dawn road transfer to Amboseli (4 hours). Check in with Kilimanjaro views. Afternoon game drive — elephant herds in the swamp, the mountain in the background.

3

Full Day Amboseli

Dawn drive for clear Kilimanjaro views and elephant herds at the water. Full day in the park with lion, cheetah, wildebeest, and zebra on the open plains.

4

Fly to Samburu

Morning game drive then fly north to Samburu (1 hour). Afternoon arrival and first drive targeting the northern specials: Grevy's zebra, reticulated giraffe, gerenuk, Beisa oryx.

5

Full Day Samburu

Full day in the northern frontier. River elephants, crocodile, lion along the Ewaso Nyiro. Optional Samburu cultural visit. The landscape here feels like a different country from the Mara.

6

Fly to Masai Mara

Morning drive then fly south to the Masai Mara (1.5 hours). Private conservancy camp check-in. Afternoon drive on private tracks — the contrast with Samburu is immediate and dramatic.

7

Full Day Masai Mara

Full day in the Mara. The culmination of the circuit. Dawn to dusk on private conservancy land — Big Five, cheetah, wild dog in season, river crossings during migration months. Fly back to Nairobi from the airstrip.
