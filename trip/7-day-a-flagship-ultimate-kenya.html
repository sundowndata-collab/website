Safari Overview

What to Expect

The Ultimate Kenya Circuit is our masterpiece format. Starting in Amboseli — where Africa's largest elephant herds move against the backdrop of Kilimanjaro — then transferring to the Masai Mara for four days of big cat tracking.

This is the safari we recommend to first-time Kenya visitors who want a complete picture. The two ecosystems are entirely different and complement each other perfectly.

Day by Day

Your Itinerary

1

Nairobi to Amboseli

Morning drive or flight to Amboseli. Afternoon arrival game drive. First elephant sightings with Kilimanjaro backdrop.

2

Full Day Amboseli

Dawn elephant tracking. Large herds, matriarchs, calves. Big Five across open plains, swamps, and acacia woodlands.

3

Amboseli to Masai Mara

Morning game drive. Midday transfer to Masai Mara. Afternoon arrival drive and ecosystem briefing.

4

Masai Mara — Big Cat Day

Full day on Mara conservancy. Lion pride tracking, leopard corridor mapping, cheetah on open plains.

5

River Crossing Positioning

Full day river crossing positioning (Jul–Oct) or predator tracking. Bush lunch in the field.

6

Private Conservancy Access

Full day exclusive private conservancy. Zero other vehicles. Maximum density wildlife.

7

Final Dawn Drive & Departure

Last pre-dawn drive. Farewell breakfast. Transfer to Nairobi.
