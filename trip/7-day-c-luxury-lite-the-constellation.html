About This Safari

The Constellation is our flagship luxury experience. Seven days covering the Masai Mara's private northern conservancy and Amboseli National Park, staying exclusively in premium tented camps with en-suite facilities, private outdoor showers, and elevated star-bed platforms for sleeping out under Kenya's unpolluted night sky. The centrepiece is a pre-dawn hot air balloon flight over the Mara at sunrise. Every detail is arranged. Every experience is private.

Ideal for: Luxury travellers, honeymoons, significant milestone birthdays or anniversaries, ultra-premium bucket list experiences.

Day by Day

1

Fly into the Mara

Wilson Airport to Mara North Conservancy — 45 minutes by private charter. Met at the airstrip, transferred to your luxury camp. Afternoon first game drive on private conservancy tracks.

2

Full Luxury Mara Day

Dawn game drive — private conservancy access means you follow the action wherever it leads. Return for full breakfast. Rest. Afternoon golden hour drive. First star-bed night under the Mara sky.

3

Hot Air Balloon Morning

Pre-dawn wake-up. One hour balloon flight at sunrise over the Mara plains. Champagne breakfast in the bush. Afternoon private game drive.

4

Second Star-Bed Night

Full day game drives including a walking safari with armed escort. Night drive after sunset. Second night on the star-bed platform — guided stargazing session with your guide.

5

Fly to Amboseli

Morning drive then fly south to Amboseli (1.5 hours). Afternoon check-in to your luxury Amboseli lodge. Kilimanjaro views. First afternoon drive with the elephant herds.

6

Full Day Amboseli

Dawn drive for the clearest Kilimanjaro visibility. Full day with the largest elephant herds in East Africa. Sunset from the lodge terrace with the mountain.

7

Final Dawn Drive & Fly Out

Pre-dawn final game drive for last light on Kilimanjaro. Champagne breakfast. Transfer to Amboseli airstrip. Fly back to Nairobi.
