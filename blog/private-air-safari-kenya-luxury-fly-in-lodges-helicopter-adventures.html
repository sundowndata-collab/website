Private Air Safari Kenya: Luxury Fly-In Lodges & Helicopter Adventures

Published: August 05, 2025  ·  Sundown Safaris Field Team

The Cessna’s wheels lift off the Mara’s dirt airstrip as your guide points below: "That’s the pride we tracked yesterday—see the cubs?" In 90 minutes, you’ll land for lunch in Laikipia, where rhinos wander past your terrace. This is the private air safari—where Kenya’s vast distances collapse into a single epic journey.

For those who refuse to waste hours on bumpy roads (or miss a sundowner because of a flat tire), here’s how to design your fly-in luxury safari.

The Itinerary Blueprint

The 5-Day Sky Safari

Day 1: Nairobi → Masai Mara (45-min flight)

  • Land at Angama Mara’s private strip by 10 AM. Game drive by noon.

Day 3: Mara → Loisaba (1-hr flight)

  • Helicopter highlight: Land on Mount Kenya’s glaciers for champagne at 15,000 ft.

Day 5: Loisaba → Lamu (2-hr flight)

  • Trade bush dust for Swahili coast villas.

Cost: ~£15,000 pp (vs. 14+ hours of driving)
