Best Time to Visit Masai Mara

Published: July 21, 2025  ·  Sundown Safaris Field Team

Timing is everything when it comes to safari. Whether you're aiming to witness the Great Migration or avoid the crowds, here's a breakdown of what each month offers in Masai Mara.

January–March

  • Dry season, fewer tourists
  • Great for big cat sightings

April–June

  • Green season with fewer vehicles
  • Stunning landscapes for photography

July–October

  • Peak season & Great Migration
  • Higher prices, but unforgettable views

November–December

  • Short rains but still great game viewing
  • Beautiful post-rain landscapes

Migration Calendar

  • Wildebeest usually arrive in Mara around July
  • River crossings peak in August and September

Budget & Crowd Considerations

  • Off-peak travel = lower rates, fewer people
  • Peak season = more options, must book early
