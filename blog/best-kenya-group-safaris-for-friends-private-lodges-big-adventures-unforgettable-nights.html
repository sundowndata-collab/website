Why Kenya is the Ultimate Group Safari Destination

✅ Private conservancies = No crowds, just your crew
✅ Exclusive-use lodges & villas – Party vibes, no strangers
✅ Adrenaline-packed activities (Think: helicopter flips over the Mara)
✅ Bonding moments – Sundowners, bush dinners, and campfire stories

Best Group Size: 6-12 people (Most luxury lodges max out at 12 for exclusivity)

Top 5 Group-Friendly Luxury Lodges

1. Angama Mara (Masai Mara) – The Instagram Dream

Why Groups Love It:

  • Private airstrip (Skip the long drives)
  • Infinity pool overlooking the Mara (Golden hour drinks, anyone?)
  • "Out of Africa" picnics – Your own private cliffside feast

Sleeps: 12 in exclusive-use villas

2. Saruni Wild (Mara North) – The Wild Party Spot

Why Groups Love It:

  • No Wi-Fi = Real connection (Board games & storytelling nights)
  • Open-air lounge with wildlife views (Sip cocktails while elephants wander by)
  • Bush sleep-outs (Fall asleep under the stars as a group)

Sleeps: 8 in luxury tents

3. Segera Retreat (Laikipia) – The Billionaire’s Playground

Why Groups Love It:

  • Private art gallery & spa (For the bougie friends)
  • Helicopter flips to Mount Kenya (Epic group photos guaranteed)
  • Zero-gravity beds (Stargazing with cocktails in hand)

Sleeps: 12 in exclusive villas
