Family Safaris in Kenya: Kid-Friendly Activities, Lodges & Packing Tips

Published: July 21, 2025  ·  Sundown Safaris Field Team

Going on safari with kids is not only possible — it’s magical. Kenya offers an incredible opportunity for children and parents alike to bond in nature while learning about wildlife and culture. Here’s how to plan the perfect family safari.

Why Kenya is Perfect for Families

  • Wide open spaces with easy wildlife spotting
  • Child-friendly lodges with family suites and swimming pools
  • Engaging activities like wildlife talks and junior ranger programs

Best Family-Friendly Lodges

  • Sarova Mara Game Camp – Family tents and activity centers
  • Porini Mara Camp – Eco-friendly and community-supported

Activities for Kids

  • Guided bush walks (age-appropriate)
  • Visits to Maasai villages with storytelling
  • Safari-themed arts and crafts

Packing Tips for Families

  • Lightweight clothing and closed shoes
  • Binoculars and wildlife guides for kids
  • Motion sickness tablets for long drives

Sample 4-Day Itinerary

  • Day 1: Arrival and relaxed afternoon
  • Day 2: Game drive and lodge-based activities
  • Day 3: Nature walk and cultural visit
  • Day 4: Final game drive and departure

Booking

  • Includes accommodation, park fees, meals, game drives
