2-Day Masai Mara Safari from Nairobi

Published: July 21, 2025  ·  Sundown Safaris Field Team

Planning a short but unforgettable safari? A 2-day Masai Mara safari from Nairobi is one of the most immersive and convenient options available, especially for travelers on a tight schedule. From breathtaking landscapes to world-renowned wildlife sightings, here's everything you need to know.

Why Choose a 2-Day Safari?

The Masai Mara National Reserve is just 5–6 hours from Nairobi by road or a quick 45-minute flight. This proximity makes it ideal for travelers who want to experience the magic of the African savannah without committing to a week-long trip. Whether you're in Kenya for business or leisure, a 2-day escape into the wild is entirely doable and deeply rewarding.

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Departure from Nairobi & Game Drive

  • Depart Nairobi by 6:00 AM
  • Scenic drive through the Great Rift Valley
  • Arrive at your lodge or tented camp by noon
  • Afternoon game drive to catch the Big Five
  • Sunset sundowner with views over the Mara plains

Day 2: Morning Game Drive & Return

  • Early morning game drive — best for predator activity
  • Breakfast at the lodge
  • Optional Maasai village visit
  • Drive or fly back to Nairobi by late afternoon

Wildlife You Can Expect to See

The Masai Mara is teeming with wildlife. You’ll have the chance to spot:

  • Lions, leopards, elephants, and rhinos
  • The elusive cheetah and African wild dog
  • Over 450 species of birds
  • Seasonal Great Wildebeest Migration (July–October)
